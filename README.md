# Task 1 – Business Sales Performance Analytics

##  Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products,
and regional performance to support business decision-making.

##  Dataset
- UK Non-Store Online Retail Dataset
- Source: Kaggle

##  Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

##  Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Revenue calculation
- Country-wise sales analysis
- Monthly revenue trend analysis
- Business insights generation

##  Key Insights
- United Kingdom contributes the highest revenue
- Clear seasonal and monthly sales trends observed
- Data cleaning significantly improves insight accuracy
